Frequently asked questions

Why?

This design system has evolved over the past few years through my work. As it reached a certain stage of maturity, I figured it wouldn't be too much extra work to package it up so other people could use it.

I was wrong. Very wrong. But it's too late to turn back now.

How is Modulz different?

Modulz tries to focus on a few differentiating factors:

  • A high standard of visual design in keeping with modern trends.
  • Comprehensive docs which illustrate not just how to implement each component, but how to use them in a variety of practical situations.
  • Consistent, rhythmic scales dictate every style used throughout Modulz.
  • A modular component library. Install only what you need.

When will Modulz be released?

If you want to try it without docs, please tweet at me and I'll hook you up.

The plan is to release a beta around mid August with v1 shipping in late September.

Can I contribute?

Please do! Modulz is on Github, so feel free to play around and open a PR or issue.

Soon, I'll compile a list of issues, tasks and ideas to help provide some direction.
